Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

💡 The Short Version

ClipStash stores all clipboard data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your clipboard contents, personal data, or usage analytics on external servers.

📋 What Data ClipStash Accesses

ClipStash accesses the following data, all of which remains on your device:

Data Purpose Stored
Clipboard contents Display your clipboard history Locally on device
Source app name Show which app a clip came from Locally on device
App preferences Remember your settings Locally on device
Trial status Manage free trial period Locally (Keychain)
Purchase status Verify Pro subscription Apple (via StoreKit)

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

ClipStash does not collect or transmit:

  • Your clipboard contents or history
  • Personal information (name, email, location)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Advertising data of any kind

💾 Local Data Storage

All clipboard history is stored in a local database on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

You can delete all stored clipboard history at any time from ClipStash Settings → Clear History. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Trial status information is stored in the macOS Keychain to prevent trial abuse. This data contains only a timestamp and persists across app installations.

💳 Subscriptions & Purchases

ClipStash offers in-app purchases managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not process, store, or have access to any payment information.

Subscription management, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple. You can manage your subscription in System Settings → Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions.

🔐 Accessibility Permission

ClipStash requests macOS Accessibility permission for two purposes:

  • Global keyboard shortcut — to register and respond to the ⌘⇧V shortcut system-wide
  • Simulated paste — to paste selected clips into the active application

This permission is used solely for these functions and does not grant ClipStash access to screen content, keystrokes, or other applications' data.

👥 Third-Party Services

ClipStash does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service interaction is with Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchase verification.

🗄️ Data Retention

Clipboard history is retained on your device until you manually delete it via Settings → Clear History, or until it is automatically replaced when the history limit is reached (10 items for free users, unlimited for Pro users).

Pinned clips are retained indefinitely until you unpin and delete them. Uninstalling ClipStash removes all locally stored clipboard data. Trial status in the Keychain persists across installations to prevent trial abuse.

⚖️ Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or process any personal data on external servers, you have full control over your information at all times:

  • Access — All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion — Clear your history from Settings, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Portability — Your data resides on your device and is accessible through standard macOS file system

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Because ClipStash does not collect, store, or process personal data on external servers, these regulations have minimal applicability. However, if you believe you have a data-related concern, please contact us.
